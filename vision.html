Vision & Passion

As the founder of Kampung Wai Cafe, Muhammad Fatihul Hakeem believes that a cafe is more than just a place to enjoy food and coffee. He dreams of creating a warm and peaceful space where people can relax, connect, and create meaningful memories together.

Through Kampung Wai Cafe, he hopes to inspire positivity, comfort, and creativity within the local community while also promoting the beauty and uniqueness of Kuala Perlis. His passion for hospitality and customer experience motivates him to continuously improve the cafe environment, services, and overall atmosphere for every visitor.

Fatihul Hakeem also believes in personal growth and lifelong learning. He hopes to become a successful entrepreneur who can inspire young people to pursue their dreams with confidence, discipline, and determination. For him, success is not only measured by achievements, but also by the positive impact he leaves on others through his journey and business.
